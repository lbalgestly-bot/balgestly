General Assembly

We live in a world where many people face stress, pain, and sadness. In these moments, light becomes a powerful symbol of hope and love. Jesus Christ is called the Light of the World because He shows us the way to truth, love, and goodness. His teachings guide us to choose what is right, even when the path seems dark. Being touched by the Light of the World also means sharing that light with others. Through kindness, forgiveness, compassion, and also respect we can reflect His light in our daily lives. When we choose to help, to understand, and to love, we bring hope to those around us. In this way, the Light of the World continues to shine, reminding us that darkness can never overcome light.
